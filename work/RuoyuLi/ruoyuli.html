Water Catchment Corridor




Work by Ruoyu Li

Introduction

The project proposes a rainwater catchment corridor and park designs in the south part of Braddock, as this certain area faces a higher probability of flooding with a lower elevation. The proposal combines linear and nodal designs to create possibilities for rainwater harvesting.

Based on an evaluation of flooding risk, the linear corridor is proposed in two phases of development, with the southern area of the project in the first phase and the northern part in the second. Three (currently vacant) lots connected by the corridors are proposed as parks with more specific ecological functions for water storage and filtration.
